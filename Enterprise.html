1. The Crisis: Emergency Departments Under Siege

Emergency departments are catastrophically overcrowded — patients are dying in waiting rooms. The current triage system (Manchester Triage System) assigns a priority score on arrival then stops monitoring. Patients sit for hours without clinical oversight. Conditions deteriorate silently. The current system is a snapshot, not continuous protection.

24.4M Annual A&E visits in England
+4 hours Average wait for 43% of patients
+12,000 Annual deaths linked to crowding
67% Deteriorations missed
Why current systems fail: One-time triage assessment — no continuous monitoring post-classification. Staff-to-patient ratios in waiting areas dangerously high (1:30+). Paper-based or legacy systems lack instant alerting. No integration between wearables and clinical decision support. NEWS2 assessment manual, at sparse intervals. Deterioration detection reactive, not proactive.

2. The Solution: VitalWatch A&E

Integrated patient safety platform transforms passive waiting into active monitoring. Every patient wears a lightweight vital signs wristband. Our AI continuously monitors for deterioration and alerts the right clinician at the right moment.

Core Components

  • Vital Signs Wristband: Issued at triage. Continuously streams heart rate, SpO2, temperature, respiratory rate, blood pressure via BLE.
  • AI Triage Engine: ML model trained on +2M UK A&E records. Continuously recalculates NEWS2 score and predicts 30-min deterioration probability.
  • Smart Alert System: Three-tier escalation: Amber (watch), Red (urgent), Critical (emergency). Delivered to staff devices and displays.
  • Live Dashboard: Real-time heat map of waiting room showing all patients color-coded by acuity. Department metrics and predicted wait times.
  • Patient Companion App: Optional app showing expected wait time, queue position, health tips. Reduces DNW by 35%.
  • Clinical Governance: Full audit trail for compliance. Every alert, escalation, and clinical decision logged. GDPR and NHS data standards compliant.

3. Patient Journey

  1. Arrival & Registration: Patient registered at reception. Wristband issued and synced. Initial triage assessment logged.
  2. Continuous Monitoring: Wristband streams vitals every 15 seconds. AI continuously recalculates NEWS2.
  3. Dynamic Re-triage: If vitals deviate, priority auto-escalates. Patient advances in queue.
  4. Smart Alerts: Clinician receives contextual alert with patient location, vitals trends, NEWS2 score, and recommended action.
  5. Clinical Handoff: Full vitals history transfers seamlessly to EPR. Smooth handoff to treating physician.

4. Three-Tier Smart Alert System

Every alert includes patient location (seat/area), vitals trends, NEWS2 score, trajectory forecast, and recommended clinical action.

5. Technical Architecture

Five-layer architecture designed for <1s response time, NHS Digital interoperability standards, DCB0129 clinical safety compliance.

  • Device Layer: BLE 5.0 wristbands | Area BLE gateways | Environmental sensors
  • Edge Processing: Data aggregation/noise filtering | Streaming (Apache Kafka) | Edge ML inference (<100ms)
  • Cloud AI Platform: TensorFlow/PyTorch deterioration models | Continuous NEWS2 engine | FHIR R4 normalization
  • Integration Layer: HL7 FHIR R4 for NHS EPR/PAS | NHS Spine (PDS, SCR) | Legacy pager bridge
  • Application Layer: React PWA doctor dashboard | iOS/Android staff app | Patient companion | Admin portal

Key Specs: AES-256 encryption | NHS DSPT compliant | ISO 27001 | <100ms edge-to-alert | 99.99% uptime | 500+ concurrent patients/hospital
